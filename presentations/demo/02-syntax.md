## Everything Markdown, plus a few extras

Regular text, **bold**, *italic*, `inline code`, [links](https://github.com) — plus lists, quotes and tables. Inline icons like :check: :star: :zap: and keys such as [[Space]] or [[Ctrl]] + [[F]].

> Quotes are highlighted with the slide's accent color.

```sql
SELECT Name, City FROM Customers
WHERE City = 'Cluj-Napoca' ORDER BY Name;
```

| GFM tables | yes :check: |
